You can manage the claimant's DWP benefits

A deputyship allows you to manage the claimant's benefits - you don't need to become an appointee.

To manage their benefits, you must send the court order to prove they have the right to act.

Check that the order gives them permission to manage property and financial matters, including the claimant’s benefits.

All court orders must include:

  • The Court of Protection valdiation stamp at the top of the first page
  • The Court of Protection seal at the bottom of the page

Please send documentation to example@dwp.co.uk.
