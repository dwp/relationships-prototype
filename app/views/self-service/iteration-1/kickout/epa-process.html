You can manage the claimant's DWP benefits

Enduring power of attorney allows you to manage the claimant's benefits - you don't need to become an appointee.

To manage their benefits, you must send evidence of the claimant's enduring power of attorney to DWP. You should send the original document to example@dwp.gov.uk.
