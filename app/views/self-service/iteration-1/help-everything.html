Managing the claimant's benefits

The claimant might be able to manage their own benefits, but needs help like:

  • adjustments when attending appointments
  • large print letters
  • someone to advocate for them for a short time

The claimant might not be able to manage their own benefits at all, and needs someone to act on their behalf. This includes someone managing their finances and attending their appointments.
