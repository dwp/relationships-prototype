You can manage the claimant's DWP benefits

A guardianship allows you to manage the claimant's benefits - you don't need to become an appointee.

To manage their benefits, the guardian must have a court order and be registered with the Office of the Public Guardian (Scotland).

All court orders must include:

  • The Court of Protection valdiation stamp at the top of the first page
  • The Court of Protection seal at the bottom of the page

Please send documentation to example@dwp.co.uk.
